How to Use

SmartLabel
Generate warehouse location labels with barcodes and arrows pointing up or down. One label per page, sized for your 12×4″ printer. Three steps: pick your arrow rules, enter your locations, hit Generate.
1

Set arrow direction by level

The last digit of a location is the level (e.g. K2-01-05-3). Pick whether each level should print an UP or DOWN arrow.

Arrow Direction (by Level)
Level 1: DOWN ▾ Level 2: DOWN ▾ Level 3: UP ▾
2

Enter your locations (pick one mode)

Three ways to give the app your locations. Use whichever is easiest.

Manual Entry Bulk Paste Import CSV
K2-01-01-1 THRU K2-01-100-3

Manual Entry — type a start and end location. The app fills everything in between.

Bulk Paste — paste many ranges at once, one per line:

K2-02-01-1 THRU K2-02-105-3
K2-03-01-1 THRU K2-03-105-3
K2-04-01-1 THRU K2-04-105-3

Import CSV — drag in a CSV with one location per row. Format: Location,Arrow (arrow = UP, DOWN, or NONE).

⬆   Drag & drop a CSV file here
Tip: CSV mode lets you control every row's arrow individually. The level rules from Step 1 are ignored when using CSV.
3

Preview & Generate

Hit Preview to see every label that will print. Once it looks right, hit Generate PDF and choose where to save it.
